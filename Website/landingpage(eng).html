Brain Blitz

In this project, we present a simple, safe and well-functioning quiz game called Brain Blitz

What is flashcard?

A flashcard is a quick way to review information to learn or memorize. Whether you're preparing for an exam or simply want to put your knowledge together, using cards can help.

Where are they available?

We can use various flashcards apps, software or even website. The difference between the two is that you need to install the software on your device, while website-based flashcards apps don't.

Why is the FlashCards app good?

With this application you can learn effectively, it displays good results and summarizes them, it is always up-to-date and has a simple interface.
